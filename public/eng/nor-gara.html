How are we?

LibroGune is a project to promote local bookstores in the Duranguesado. Our goal is to encourage small booksellers in the region by making the public aware of the bookstores that are located around them.

On our website you can find information about all the bookstores in the Duranguesado: where they are, what they offer, when they open and interesting facts about them. In this way, you will be able to help the local trade when buying books.

For us, local bookstores are not just stores; they are the cultural centers of our community where readers meet and conversations arise. With your support, you help keep these alive.

Our Mission

To publicize and promote local bookstores in the Durangaldea, in order to strengthen the local economy and culture.

Our values

Commitment to local commerce, promotion of culture and strengthening of community ties.

Our Vision

To turn the Durangaldea Bookstore into a reference space of strong and plural local character.
